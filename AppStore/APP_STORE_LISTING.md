## Description

**Reconnect with Reality**

Grounded is your companion for managing Depersonalization-Derealization Disorder (DPDR). Whether you're experiencing episodes of feeling disconnected from yourself or the world around you, Grounded provides clinically-informed tools to help you feel present again.

**What is DPDR?**

Depersonalization-Derealization Disorder is a dissociative condition where you feel detached from yourself (depersonalization) or your surroundings (derealization). You might feel like you're watching your life from outside your body, or that the world seems foggy and unreal. Grounded is specifically designed to help you manage these symptoms.
